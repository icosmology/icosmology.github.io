Welcome to GB's cosmos!


I am Gong-Bo Zhao (赵公博), who studies the Universe for a living. I was born at redshift z=7.5E-11 on earth (39° 40' N, 117° 45' E) with a lensing mass of 1.6 E-30 solar mass, and have grown 20 times in mass following a highly nonlinear structure formation. I speak Mandarin, English, Fortran, C/C++, Python and Julia. I like things that are simple but profound, for example, Zen and our Universe!


I am a full professor at NAOC, and a lead scientist of the cosmology and gravitation group. I was educated at here for undergrad and MSc in theoretical physics, and here for a Ph.D in cosmology. I did my postdocs at here and here, before joining NAOC as a faculty member.
